Steps to Manually Deactivate Tenant

  1. Please read all the steps in the deactivation sequence. Once you press "Done", you will be logged out and no longer able to access this screen.
  2. Navigate to the Classic Azure Management portal and click on Active Directory.
  3. Select the directory containing the service catalog application.
  4. Click on "Applications" then select the service catalog you would like to deactivate.
  5. Select "Manage Access" on the bottom of the window.
  6. Finally, click on "Remove access" to remove the catalog.
